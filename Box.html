Cat

A cat is a small and friendly animal that many people keep as a pet.
It has soft fur, bright eyes, and sharp claws.
Cats like to play, climb, and sleep for many hours during the day.
They are very clean and often groom themselves.

Rabbit

A rabbit is a small and gentle animal that is often kept as a pet.
It has soft fur, long ears, and a short fluffy tail.
Rabbits like to hop around, chew on vegetables, and dig in the ground.
They are social animals and enjoy the company of other rabbits.

Dog

A dog is a loyal and friendly animal that is often kept as a pet.
It has a strong sense of smell, sharp hearing, and a playful nature.
Dogs like to run, fetch balls, and go for walks with their owners.
They are social animals and enjoy the company of other dogs.
